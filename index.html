About Me

Born on August 2nd, 1957 in Senglea, Malta, Domenic has been involved in the photojournalism sphere since his early twenties.

Senior official Malta football writer / photojournalist correspondent to UEFA.com â€“ the official site of the Union of European Football Associations - for 23 years to date, and a regular photographic contributor to various UEFA online and published contributions.

A self-taught seasoned all-round photographer, Domenic's association with UEFA spans a long number of years. Specializing in photo reportage, the Malta-based photographer has covered 18 UEFA Champions League finals to date, plus numerous UEFA Champions League matches and other UEFA official matches. His photojournalistic contributions to UEFA.com and various UEFA publications have been numerous and still ongoing.

The Malta Falcon

In January 2023, Domenic launched his personal autobiography "The Malta Falcon", donating all proceeds to Hospice Malta, an NGO which provides the highest standards of palliative care on the island free of charge.
